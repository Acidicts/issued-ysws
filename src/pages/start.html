Get Started

Step 1: Design

Create a design themed around hackclub or coding using our editor. Make sure it's unique and represents your style!

Step 2: Submit

Submit your design to our platform. You can use the submission form available on our website.

Step 3: Vote

Vote for your favorite designs. The top 5 designs with the most votes will be featured in our store.

Step 4: Order

Once the top designs are announced, you can order apparel featuring those designs directly from our store.
